Event Quest: Challenge Simulator SSS-Rank - Lantern-Type Kaibutsu

Challenge Simulator SSS-Rank - Lantern-Type Kaibutsu
Duration
Main Quests15 Oct 2025 14:00 JST to 13 Nov 2025 14:00 JST
News link
  • Campaign Details
  • Quest Details

Campaign Details

Starting Wednesday, October 15, 2025, at 2:00 PM, a new difficulty level, “SSS-Rank,” will be available for a limited time in the Challenge Simulator.

“Challenge Simulator SSS-Rank - Lantern-Type Kaibutsu” is an ultra-high difficulty score attack challenge for seasoned Observers.

In this score attack quest, your score is calculated based on the battle’s outcome at the end, and you receive rewards according to that score. You can achieve a higher score by clearing the battle in fewer turns or dealing more damage to the enemy.

Even if you lose the battle, your score will be calculated, and you will receive rewards based on that score.

“Challenge Simulator SSS-Rank - Lantern-Type Kaibutsu” has 0 stamina cost, allowing you to play it anytime and as many times as you wish during the event period.

Challenge this formidable foe and earn rewards including Ether Stone!

出現エネミー：灯篭型カイブツ

詳細：
【灯篭型カイブツ】は特殊なゲージを所持しており、ダメージを受けた時やスキル1使用時にゲージが増加し、灯篭型カイブツがスキル2やスキル3使用した時と味方がView獲得スキルを使用した時にゲージが減少する。

【灯篭型カイブツ】はゲージが最大の時、ターン終了時に強力な全体攻撃を行う。

スキル1使用時とゲージ最大時の全体攻撃を行う度に、【灯篭型カイブツ】のATKは増加していく。

【灯篭型カイブツ】はピンチ状態の時、ダメージ時のゲージの増加量が上昇する他、スキル2で敵全体に確率で過消費を付与するようになる。

【灯篭型カイブツ】は火属性。水属性のアタッカ―で挑もう！
