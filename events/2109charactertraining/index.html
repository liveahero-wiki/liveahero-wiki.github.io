Event Quest: Character Training Campaign

Character Training Campaign
Duration
Main Quests16 Sep 2021 18:00 JST to 30 Sep 2021 14:00 JST
News link

Event Details

For a limited time there will be special training quest, consisting of two ranks (A and B) with three kinds of rewards (Exp, Laf, Training Band) for a total of 6 quests.

  • [A Rank] Hero Training! Exp Item
  • [B Rank] Hero Training! Exp Item
  • [A Rank] Hero Training! Lah (Coin)
  • [B Rank] Hero Training! Lah (Coin)
  • [A Rank] Hero Training! Training Band
  • [B Rank] Hero Training! Training Band

Each first clear will grant 1 Ether Stone.

Also, there will be daily missions and limited time missions for character training campaign, rewarding Ether Ticket and Legendary Medal.

Lastly, together with this campaign we will get our next challenge quest, which is Practice! Bar Security Training! (Procy), this challenge quest will have 3 quests and clearing all of them will rewards 1 Ether Ticket.

Daily Missions:

5 Clear [Training Mission!] daily mission 25 times in total 3 Stamina Drink
