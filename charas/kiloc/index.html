Personal Data 1

ヒーローに変身したキロクは、艦長らしい軍服型のヒーロースーツで戦場に臨む。肩に担がれたアンカー型のパラレルウェポンは投擲武器にもなるが、それは本来の用途ではない。艦が目指すべき場所に向かって放たれたアンカーは、艦を傍に留めるという目的を逆説的に果たす。それはつまり、アンカーが到達した地点まで艦にバリアを展開し、クルーを安全に導くことを意味する。しかし、アンカー自体を撃ち落とされると能力の真価を発揮できないという欠点と表裏一体の性質であり、運用には適切な状況判断が不可欠である。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

In the battlefield Kiloc transform into a military-style hero suit resembling that of a captain.

The anchor-shaped parallel weapon slung over his shoulder can also be used as a throwing weapon, but that is not its primary purpose.

The anchor, when fired towards the destination the ship should reach, paradoxically serves the purpose of keeping the ship nearby. This means that when the anchor reaches its target, a barrier is deployed around the ship to safely guide the crew.

However, the anchor’s true effectiveness cannot be realized if it is shot down, and this flaw is intertwined with its nature. Therefore, proper situational judgment is essential for its use

Personal Data 2

エステボルタの先代総艦長を父に持つキロクは、都市艦隊を運用するための英才教育を幼い頃から叩き込まれた。しかし厳格で仕事熱心な一方、家族を顧みない父に反感を抱いたキロクは、父の望む息子像からはかけ離れた人物に成長していく。ろくに働きもしないが気さくで明朗快活な彼のことを、エステボルタの人々は「遊び人の放蕩息子」と揶揄しながらも憎めない人物と評していた。そんな彼が艦長に就任し、ヒーローとして掲げるのは「争いのない凪の海へと皆を導く」という普段の言動とはかけ離れたもの。しかし、その胸中を知るものはごく一部に限られる。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

Kiloc, the son of the former supreme fleet commander of Estevolta, was subjected to an elite education from a young age to learn how to operate the urban fleet.

However, despite his father being a strict and hardworking person, Kiloc developed a sense of resentment towards him for neglecting his family.

As a result, Kiloc grew into a person who was far removed from the ideal son his father had envisioned. He was a carefree, cheerful, and outgoing person who hardly worked, and though the people of Estevolta would tease him as the playboy, reckless son, they couldn’t help but find him likable.

When he eventually became the captain, the hero he presented himself to be, aim to lead everyone to a peaceful, calm sea without conflict, was far removed from his usual demeanor.

However, only a select few knew the true thoughts behind his actions.

Side Story

ムイラウカⅣ最大の都市艦隊・エステボルタで、すべての艦を統べる「総艦長」の役職に就く男性。「ムイラウカ星系一のお気楽者」の呼び名に違わぬ奔放な人物であり、だらしなく軟派で非常に好色。同艦隊都市の航海士を務める養子・ジョウゼンとは、どちらが保護者か分からないようなやり取りを日夜繰り広げている。そんな人物がエステボルタの艦長に就任した背景には、ムイラウカⅣにおける統括組織「艦長会議」の暗躍があったのではないかと、まことしやかに囁かれているが憶測の域を出ない。

Side Story (Translated)

A man who holds the position of Supreme Fleet Commander, the highest-ranking officer overseeing all the ships of Estevolta, the largest urban fleet in the Muirauqa IV.

True to his nickname as the most carefree person in Muirauqa, he is an uninhibited, easygoing individual who is lazy, flirtatious, and highly lecherous.

He engages in daily interactions with his adopted son, Joezen, who serves as the fleet’s navigator, to the point where it is unclear who is the guardian and who is the dependent.

The background behind this man’s appointment as the commander of Estevolta is rumored to involve the covert actions of the governing organization of Muirauqa IV, the Council of Commanders, though this remains nothing more than speculation.
