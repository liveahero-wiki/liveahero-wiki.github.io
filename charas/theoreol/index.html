Personal Data 1

テオレオールは長い年月で己の肉体に様々な物体を取り込んできた。吸収し、溶かされたその物体は情報としてテオレオールの肉体に蓄積し、錬金術の触媒として使用される。テオレオールの用いる実験器具のパラレルウェポンは錬金術における様々な工程を極限まで最適化するもの。このパラレルウェポンを用いることでテオレオールは自身の知る状況に応じた物質を、瞬時に生成することが可能となる。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Theoreol has incorporated numerous objects into his body over the years. The absorbed and melted substances accumulate in Theoreol’s body as information and are used as material for alchemy. The laboratory equipment parallel weapon used by Theoreol optimizes various processes in alchemy to their utmost limit. By using this parallel weapon, Theoreol can instantly generate various substances he knows the chemical composition of according to the situation.

Personal Data 2

テオレオールは己を「出来損ない」と呼称する。それはテオレオールを錬成した錬金術師が、あえて「不完全なもの」として錬成したからに他ならない。創造者はなぜこんな出来損ないを錬成したのか、不完全な生命たる己の存在意義は何なのか。創造者が研究の果てに目指していた錬金術師の理想、完全の象徴たる「賢者の石」の錬成。これを成せばその意味がわかるのだろうか。その仮説を実証するため、長い長い年月、テオレオールはただ独りで錬金術の研究を続けている。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

Theoreol calls himself “a failure.” It is because the alchemist who created him declared him so. An “imperfect” specimen. Why did the Creator forge such a failure, and what is the significance of his existence as an imperfect life? The alchemist’s ideal that his Creator aimed for at the end of his research, the creation of the “philosopher’s stone” is his symbol of perfection. If he could succeed in creating it, will he understand the purpose of his existence? To prove his hypothesis, Theoreol has been studying alchemy alone for many, many years.

Side Story

全身を覆う衣服、顔を隠す独特の意匠が施された仮面。大抵のヒトが二度見するほどの怪しげな風体を持つ錬金術師。その正体は滅びた星の錬金術師に錬成された、スライムのような肉体を持つホムンクルス（人工生命体）である。相当に長い年月を生きており、故郷の星をなくした後もただ独り各地を放浪し、錬金術の研究を続けている。研究以外への興味が薄く、ヒトとほぼ接したことがないためか、少々一般常識に疎い。

Side Story (Translated)

Theoriol wears clothing that covers his entire body, as well as a unique mask that hides his face. Most people do a double take when they see such an odd alchemist like him for the first time. He is a homunculus, whose slime-like body was created by an alchemist on a ruined planet.

He has lived for a very long time, and even after the destruction of his planet, he continues to wander on his own and study alchemy. He has little interest in anything outside of alchemy, and because of that, he has made very little contact with people, making his knowledge of common courtesy very lacking.

Notes

  • His name is probably comes from ancient greek, θεωρέω (Theoreo) meaning: to theorize, to speculate, to observe. The origin of western alchemy is said to date back to ancient Egypt and ancient Greece.
  • His Skill name is from Latin language:
    • Extractum : Extract, the English name “alchemy” is thought to be derived from the Arabic word “خيمياء” (al kimiya). Some believe that “al” is the definite article (English “the”) and “kimiya” is derived from the ancient Greek word “χημεία” (khemeia), “the art of extracting juice”.
    • Aqua Vitae : Water of life, It was called “water of life” because it was considered by alchemists to be a medicinal drink with life-sustaining effects and a “medicine of immortality”.
    • Lapis Philosophorum : Philosopher’s stone, An elixir thought by medieval European alchemists to be a catalyst in the transformation of base metals such as lead into gold.
