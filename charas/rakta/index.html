Personal Data 1

白衣を纏ったヒーロー「ドクトル・R」に変身する。メスや鉗子といった手術道具の形をとるパラレルウェポンを複数所持、それらを駆使して戦闘や救助活動を行う。彼の掲げる「救える命は必ず救う」というモットーにより、彼の道具は例え敵であっても殺生を行うことは出来ない仕様になっている。その為、彼のヒーローとしての能力は「危機が及んでいる人々を救う」場合において真価を発揮する。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

He transforms into the white-clad hero “Doktor R”. Possessing several parallel weapons in the form of surgical instruments such as scalpels and forceps, which he can use in combat and rescue activities. Due to his motto, “All lives that can be saved must be saved,” his tools are designed to prevent him from killing even his enemies. As such, his heroic abilities shows its true value in “saving other people”.

Personal Data 2

パラレルウェポンのメスは物質間の空間を切り裂いて、対象内部とのトンネルを作り出す仕様である為、切り裂いた対象にダメージを与えることは無い。縫合すれば元通りである。変身中の彼が握る道具たちが、他者を傷つけたり、命を奪ってしまったりすることは決して無い。これは彼のヒーローであり続けるための前提であり、誓いである。戦闘に於いてもそれは変わらない為、一種の制約となる。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

His Parallel Weapon’s scalpel is designed to cut through the space between materials to create a tunnel inside the target, it does not damage the target it cuts through. Once stitched up, they are back to normal. The tools he holds in his hands while transforming will never hurt or kill others. This is his prerequisite and oath to continue being a hero. A restraint of sorts so that he remains the same even in the battlefield.

Personal Data 1

ラクタは避暑地として有名な都に派遣医師として訪れる。そこで目にしたのは薬効のある浴場で身体を、美しい故郷の風景で心を健康に保つ人々だった。その姿に心を打たれ、身体を治療するだけでなく心の健康も守る医師であろうという誓いを胸に、新たな装いを得る。その手に携えた水瓶型のパラレルウェポンからは都の美しい風景を育む水が迸る。その水は触れるものに活力を与え、水面に咲き乱れる花々は見るものの心を奮い立たせる。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Rakta went to visit a city famous as a summer retreat as a doctor. What he saw there were people who kept themselves healthy in bathhouses that healed the body while using the beautiful scenery around them to heal their minds. He was so awestruck so much by this, that he vowed to become a doctor that will heal not only the body, but also the mind, which in turn earned him a new hero form. The water jug in his hands is his parallel weapon which gushes forth water that can nurture the city’s beautiful scenery. Those who touch the water feel more revitalized, while the flowers that bloom on the water’s surafce soothe the minds of those who look at them.

Personal Data 2

研修医時代のラクタが担当した患者に、どれだけ身体が健康になってもベッドから起き上がることができないヒトがいた。故郷をなくしたというそのヒトに笑顔を取り戻せなかったことを、ラクタはずっと悔んでいた。患者が自分の意思で立ち上がれるまで寄り添い、心と体の健康を取り戻す。かつてそのヒトが渇望した故郷のように、樹や花が生い茂る景色を育む水瓶を掲げるラクタの顔は、医師としての新たな決意に満ちていた。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

One of the patients that Rakta was in charge of during his residency was a person who could not get out of bed no matter how healthy his body became. Regardless of the health of his body, his spirit was wracked with despair by the destruction of his home and the loss of everything he had cared about. Rakta had always regretted not being able to bring hope back to the man. Swearing to accompany patients until they can stand up of their own volition and regain their mental and physical health, his parallel weapon’s flowing water nurtured a landscape of trees and flowers, like the home that patient once loved. He is filled with renewed determination as a doctor.

Side Story

月面都市の総合病院で執刀する外科医。アジア系月面人で、フルネームはラクタ・シャーリヤ。医師として非常に真面目な人物であり、その手腕にも一定の信頼を得ているものの、何をするにも生真面目であるがゆえに熱意が空回りしがち。先輩医師に「外科医は体力勝負」と教わってから、様々な健康法を試し、週５でジムに通う、何か間違った方向の努力を惜しまない健康オタクの側面がある。

Side Story (Translated)

A surgeon working at a general hospital in an immigrant city on the moon.

An Asian Lunarian whose full name is Rakta Salya.

As a doctor, he is an exceedingly serious person. Even though a fixed amount of trust in his abilities, his enthusiasm tends to run at idle because he is serious in everything he does.

After being taught by a senior doctor that “Being a surgeon is a trial of strength”, he has undergone various health methods and attend the gym 5 times a week, which is an aspect of a health nut who spares no effort even when misguided.
