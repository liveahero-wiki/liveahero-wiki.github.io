Personal Data 1

マントの下には重く厚い特殊防護スーツが装備されており、大きな衝撃を感知すると内部がエーテルで満たされることで保護される仕組みになっている。また、彼のパラレルウェポンは把手の付いた盾であり、敵の攻撃を弾き返すことや受け流すことを得意とする。体勢が崩れた相手に対しては、エネルギーで形成されたブレードでそのスキを打つ戦法を取る。使用者の本質が表れるパラレルウェポンにおいて、対象の保護に特化した「守る為の攻撃」を具現化している。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Underneath the cloak is a heavy, thick special protective suit, which is designed to protect the body by filling the interior with a corresponding ether when a large impact is detected.
His parallel weapon is a shield with a grip, which is good at repelling and deflecting enemy attacks.
If an opponent loses his stance, he uses his energy-formed blade to take advantage of their weakness.
The parallel weapon is a reflection of the user’s true nature, and his weapon specialized in the protection of his target, attacking for the sake of protecting.

Personal Data 2

若かりし頃にフェインの父親は蒸発し、残された弟と二人生き残るため苦しい生活を余儀なくされた。弟は衰弱し、ある出来事によって命を落としてしまう。弟を守るだけの力さえあれば死なせることはなかったという強い思いが、彼を「弱者を守り抜く」ヒーローとして覚醒させた。しかしその日から、弟を守れず見殺し同然にした自分には己の為に力を振るうことは許されるのかという意識が、彼の内側では答えのない呪縛のように渦巻いている。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

When he was still young, his father one day disappeared, he and his little brother are forced to lead a hard life to survive.
His brother was weak, and one day a certain incident took his life.
If only he was stronger, then his brother won’t die, holding this feeling strongly, he awakened to be a hero that protect the weak.
However ever since that day, having failed to save his brother, he no longer use his power for his own sake,
the question of whether he is allowed to wield his power for himself is swirling around inside him like an unanswerable curse.

Side Story

マセリマオセⅢスラム街出身の傭兵。警護や人命救助、対人制圧を専門に活動しており、弱者を守り抜くという意志の元、「ヒトを守る」という点においての評判は非常に高い。日頃は言葉数が少なく、護衛対象には棘のある発言が目立つが、そのどれもが過度な心配から来るものであり、戦場に付き添わせないよう頑張って嫌われようとしている様子。昔、弟が好んでいた飴玉をいつも持ち歩き、空腹を訴える者にはそれを分け与えている場面が何度も目撃されている。

Side Story (Translated)

A mercernary that hail from the slums of Maslimaoce III.
He specializes in security, lifesaving, and antipersonnel suppression and has a very good reputation in terms of protecting people based on his will to protect the weak.
A man with few words, due to his excessive concerns he try to show prickly attitude and make his target of protection hates him, so that they won’t follow him to the battlefield.
He always carry a candy that his brother used to like, and have given it many times to anyone who is hungry.

  • Muliphein is a star in the Canis Major constellation, also known as Gamma Canis Majoris
  • Muliphein is derived from Arabic word, محلفين muħlifayn, meaning jurors.
