Personal Data 1

億万長者を目指したかつての願望が強く出た、金銭を稼ぐことに特化した珍しいヒーローへと変身する。担いでいるハンマーは身体の周囲を飛び回る小型のロボットとセットで１つのパラレルウェポン。小型ロボットはその場で最も利益の上がる行動をプロキーに伝え、その状況に特化した形に変形したハンマーを以てプロキーに一攫千金のチャンスを与える、いわば「打ち出の小づち」である。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Procy transforms into an unusual hero specializing in making money, born from his strong desire to become a millionaire. The hammer he wields is part of a parallel weapon set, along with a tiny robot that flies around nearby. The robot tells Procy the most profitable course of action at any given moment, and gives Procy a chance to make a fortune with a hammer that transforms its shape to be more suitable for any given situation.

Personal Data 2

明日をも知れない貧困に喘ぐ環境で生まれ育った彼は、何不自由なく暮らす億万長者になる事を夢見て、鉱山で採掘作業員をしながら暮らしていた。そんな時に起こったカイブツの襲来の折に、振るっていた木槌が打ち出の小づちたるパラレルウェポンに変わり、ヒーローとなったことで彼の生活は一変。自らの生活を一変させられるかもしれない大きな「賭け」へと、彼は人生の舵を切ったのだ。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

Born and raised in a poverty-stricken environment in which having a tomorrow was never guaranteed, he lived as a hard laborer in a mine, all while dreaming of becoming a millionaire who could live comfortably. At that time, when the Kaibutsu attacked, the mallet he was carrying changed to a small parallel weapon, and his life changed completely. Realizing he could leverage his power as hero to make a fortune, he bets it all on one big gamble to change his life for the better.

Personal Data 1

一人のヒーローとして、頼られるべきバンチョーとして、道理を通してみせると決意を固めた姿のプロキー。これまで素直になり切れずに見落としてきた誰かの想いと正面から向き合うという意思がその学生服のようなヒーロースーツに表れている。貧しかった学生時代に夢を見た一攫千金の億万長者。未だ到達は出来ぬ中、しかし、プロキーはその先を見据え始めた。荒くれ者の友人たちと交わして鍛えたその拳で、今度は先陣を切り、仲間を守る為にその力を発揮する。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Procy took the form as a hero and a reliable bancho with steel resolution.
His willingness to face head-on the thoughts and feelings of someone he had overlooked because of his lack of honesty is reflected in his hero suit, which resembles a school uniform.
He dreamed of becoming a wealthy billionaire when he was a poor student. While he has yet to achieve it, Procy has started to look beyond it.
With his fist trained while having scuffles with his rowdy friends, he now takes the lead and uses this strength to protect his friends.

Personal Data 2

プロキーがスラムを出て、金稼ぎの旅に出ると決意した日。そこには命を救ってもらったお礼の気持ちを込めてマフラーを贈るある少年の姿があった。お世辞にも綺麗とは言えないボロボロのマフラーを、少年はどんな想いで差し出していたのか――見返りを求めないヒーローたちが掲げていた思いを理解した今。あの日受け取れなかったマフラーをその首にたなびかせ、プロキーは誰よりも速く仲間の元へと駆け付け、その拳を構える。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

One day, Procy decided to leave the slums, and determined to embark on a journey to earn money.
There he found a boy waiting for him, he gave him a muffler, as a thanks for saving his life.
With what kind of feelings did the boy give that tattered muffler to him… right now he kind of understand the thoughts inside the heroes who never ask anything in return.
With the scarf he didn’t receive that day fluttering around his neck, Procy rushes to his friends faster than anyone else, his fists ready.

Side Story

パラレルフライト社近傍にある、ヒーロー達の情報交換所兼酒場「ヒーローズギルド・アースキャンプ」店主。小型犬のような可愛らしい外見ながら、20年以上のキャリアを持つベテランヒーローであり、ライキ・ハックルとは昔馴染み。気さくで面倒見がよい男ではあるが、金銭への執着は誰よりも強い。余計な金払いを嫌い、儲け話には何かと食いつく。酒場の開店費用に結構な借金を負った様子。

Side Story (Translated)

An alien who runs “The Guild of Heroes : Earth Camp”, and information exchange center and bar for heroes.

Located near the company where the protagonist works at. Despite looking like a cute little puppy, he’s a veteran hero with more than 20 years of career over his shoulder. He is old friends with Ryekie and Huckle.

Although he is caring and open hearted man, his attachment to money is stronger than anything. Ever.

He hates excessive payments and very much prefers get-rich-quick schemes one way or another.

It seems like the bar’s opening cost has him up to his neck in debt.

Notes

  • His full name is Procyon Floud (プロキオン・フラウド)
  • Procyon, also known as Alpha Canis Minoris, is the brightest star of the constellation Canis Minor, with Gomeisa (Beta Canis Minoris) being the second brightest star there.
  • Procyon also refers to the genus of the same name that contains the three species of raccoons.
  • The name “Procyon” comes from Greek and translates to “before the dog”.
  • Both he and Shoen have a similar circular marking on their bodies; Procy has it on his forehead while Shoen has it on his abdomen.
