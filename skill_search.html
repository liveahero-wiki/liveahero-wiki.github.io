Skill labelling is still work-in-progress, there may be some inaccuracies.
Known issues / limitation
If a label is associated with a hidden passive skill, sometime there may be extra hero skills shown, even though actually only one of the active hero skill's description is associated with the label.
E.g. When filtering with "Scaling damage (Combo)", all 3 hero skills of Rastaban will be shown, even though only Skill 3 grants "Battle Cry" that scale damage by Combo.
