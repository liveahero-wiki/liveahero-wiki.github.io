## Event Preview

A ceremony celebrating the 200th anniversary of the birth of the Heroes
is being held at the Archimedes Colony on the Moon.

Marking this milestone year, a special event has been announced:
the Public Hero License Examination!

Hoping to witness the birth of new heroes, heroes from Archimedes itself,
Parallel Flight, and even from inside and outside the Earth Sphere
are gathering in great numbers for the ceremony.

However, just as the exam is about to begin,
your device starts behaving strangely…
and suddenly you’re transported somewhere completely different!?

As you search for the cause of this warp phenomenon,
you encounter a spacesuit-clad boy who dreams of adventure,
a young bounty hunter staking everything on earning his license,
and a former astronaut–turned–hero who came to help as an examiner!
On top of that, a combat-obsessed bodyguard and an antiquities-dealer operator—
a villain duo, of all things!?

Motives and actions intertwine at the lunar ceremony.
And when everything finally connects,
the overlapping stories will be revealed—
