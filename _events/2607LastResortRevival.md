## Event Preview

A swimsuit calendar project launches in midsummer Enoshima!?

Enoshima, an island to the east of Orient City. Once a thriving observatory base, it has been completely left behind by the changing times.

To bring back its former glory, a town hall employee who loves his hometown steps up.
His plan: "Local Heroes Grand Gathering in Enoshima!" Calling for support through crowdfunding, the response far exceeds expectations...!?

Invited to the project as an Operator, you end up helping with the production of the local Hero swimsuit calendar alongside an ever-positive locksmith and a strict instructor "Santa"!

As the project storms ahead, the father and son in charge of the Muirauqa IV city fleet, the heroes who protected the Lepka III emigrant ship, and the local hero "Enoshiman" all appear, creating a hero sensation on this quiet island! Will the swimsuit calendar see completion amidst the chaos of Enoshima!?

This summer in Enoshima, you are about to witness what a "true local Hero" is—
