## Event Preview

In a calm, peaceful atmosphere, the Parallel Flight office welcomes the New Year.
Just then, their very first job of the year comes rushing in—from a flustered Huckle.
The task is—making New Year’s greeting rounds!?

Together with a big-hearted and straightforward sake brewery owner you met through Huckle,
you head out to Orient City, bustling with New Year celebrations.
With cups of celebratory sake in hand, you take on your first mission of the year:
to successfully make the rounds of hero offices for the New Year’s party—
