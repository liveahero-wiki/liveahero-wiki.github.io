## Event Preview

“DO YOU WANT THE STRONGEST CACAO?!”

The legendary rock cacao “Eternal Amber,” said to be discovered only once every hundred years.
With this ingredient—coveted by every chef on the planet—on the line, the Gourmet Fighters announce the opening of the
“Cosmic Cacao Championship”!!

A timid young chocolatier and a hot-blooded pastry chef girl decide to enter in pursuit of cacao…
but they don’t even have enough members!
You, having chosen to support them as an on-site operator, rope in
a sharp, fairness-obsessed lawyer and a lazy librarian,
forming a hastily assembled team and storming the venue together!

The stage of the cooking competition is Ceremia, a forest planet overgrown with colossal trees.
As the group tackles the assigned challenges amid the mysterious woods,
they’re confronted by teams of chefs led by renowned gourmands,
as well as a militant maid brigade standing in their way!

And then, the appearance of the villain organization “Sweets Gang,” which pillages ingredients without restraint,
throws Planet Ceremia into a storm of chaos—

Who will conquer the cooking competition
and claim “Eternal Amber” for themselves?!
