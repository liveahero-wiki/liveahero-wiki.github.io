## Event Preview

The year is drawing to a close, with Christmas just around the corner.
You are visiting the frontier planet Muirauka I at the request of a pair of sibling space hunters.

No sooner have you finished the job and taken a breather than a distress signal, heavy with static, reaches the space hunters.

“If this signal has reached you… please! Lend us your strength!”

The source is the frozen frontier planet Muirauka VI.
What greets you there, amid the lingering stench of a great war, is the sender of the distress call: a boastful pioneer corps leader.

His settlers and their territory have been attacked by the mysterious invaders known as the “Spectral Army,” and now lie in a state of utter devastation.

Together with a trainee hunter’s hound boy hero who is thrilled beyond measure at seeing snow for the first time,
and a serious, earnest trainee hunter boy hero,
you launch a grand operation to save the pioneer corps on the brink of annihilation.

A misfortune-prone hunter who emerges from the wreckage of a crashed spaceship is swept up in the chaos as well,
and the battle to reclaim the settlers’ “family” members
begins to unfold in an unexpected direction…

Will you be able to welcome the long-awaited festival day known as “Thawing Christmas,”
with the entire “family” together at last?
