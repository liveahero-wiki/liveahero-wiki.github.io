## Event Preview

From Frontier Node to Zero Trust.
No abnormalities today. Construction is progressing as scheduled.

The regular transmissions that had been sent consistently from a distant galaxy suddenly ceased—
and with that final message, all communication from Frontier Node,
the massive space station owned by Zero Trust Corporation, was lost.

At the direct request of Zero Trust’s CEO, Houley,
Parallel Flight Company takes on the mission.
Akashi, you, and the company’s president, Huckle—who has decided to go in person—
set out on an investigation whose full scope remains unknown.

Joining forces on-site with a Zero Trust researcher,
an android who dreams of becoming human,
a prosthetist returned from the battlefield,
and another Akashi—
the heroes begin their investigation of Frontier Node, now an isolated island in space.

Cut off from all external communication, surrounded by a stifling sense of confinement,
and faced with an investigation that refuses to go smoothly—
still, the heroes press on without breaking.

But an ominous shadow of Kaibutsu begins to creep closer.

And then, a mysterious phenomenon that appears alongside light—
“Echo”—
begins to steer events in an unexpected direction…
