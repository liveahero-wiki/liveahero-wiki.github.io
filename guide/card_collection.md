## About Card Collection

Starting from client version v4.12.0, the new "Hero Collection Cards (ヒーローコレクションカード)" and "My Desk(マイデスク)" features will be implemented.

> Please Note: As the UI for this feature is currently only available in Japanese, the original Japanese text has been included in this description for your reference.

### Feature Overview

Through the Hero Collection Cards feature, you can obtain collection cards for various characters.
In addition to enjoying the card illustrations, collection cards allow you to view profile information such as height, weight, birthday, favorite things, and how they spend their time off.

## How to Obtain Collection Cards

Collection Cards can be obtained through the following methods:

### Obtain Card Packs from the Shop

"Card Pack(カードパック)" can be exchanged for Friend Points in the Shop.
By using a Card Pack in the "Card Search(カードサーチ)" section of "My Desk feature," you can obtain 5 Collection Cards.

### Daily Free Search

In "My Desk," you can obtain 1 Collection Card for free once per day without consuming any items.

### Viewing and Upgrading Collection Cards

From the newly established "My Desk" screen, you can check your current collection cards and upgrade them using "Card Pieces."
Gather your favorite Hero Collection Cards and build your very own ultimate collection!
